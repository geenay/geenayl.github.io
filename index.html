design process

UX DESIGN

Research | Wireframes | Prototype | Testing

I start a project by identifying the problem at hand. I find the necessary information I need from stakeholders. After conducting user research, interviews and surveys, I begin to devise strategic solutions in the form of sitemaps, wireframes and prototypes. I test the usability of my design and iterate upon them to ensure a great user experience.

UI DESIGN

Design | Testing | Branding | Interaction

I find the right brand language that forms the unique UI elements for each product. I make creative design decisions in order to best translate any given UX wireframe into a smooth and intuitive interaction. I often communicate my design through animated and interactive prototypes and prepare the necessary files for the development team.
